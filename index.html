About me

I'm a first-year CS graduate student at UCLA. My current research interest focuses on multimodal machine learning and healthcare/bio-medicine. As the nature of human communication is multimodal, I aspire to get computers to reach new heights in their understanding and open up new avenues for AI in healthcare.

I have been pondering how machines can break out of their computational boundaries to understand human intelligence. My goal is to develop computationally efficient machine learning as well as deep learning models and algorithms, building the computational foundations to enable computers with the abilities to analyze, recognize and predict subtle human communicative behaviors during social interactions. Applications include computer vision, natural language processing, audio signal processing, and drug discovery.

I love open-source community and making contributions to the open-source world, like GitHub. In Gitstar Ranking, I rank Top 0.03% of all GitHub developers (more than 37,000,000) by GitHub stars.
